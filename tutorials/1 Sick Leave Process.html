Sick Leave Process

First Day of Sick Leave

  • Inform Silke or Johannes (via email, WhatsApp, or phone call)
  • Fill in the web form

If sick for only three days: no sick note needed.

From the fourth day on: a sick note is required. The note can be issued retroactively.

Note: Weekends count as sick days.

Example: Sick from Thursday (1st day) to Sunday (4th day) â†’ Sick note needed. See your doctor by Friday.

Using the Web Form

  1. Change to English (top of page)
  2. Click "Start"
  3. Enter your last name, press return, and choose your name (auto-filled)
  4. Choose options from the dropdown menu
  5. Enter email address
  6. Select "Sickness Notification" or "Healthy Notification"
  7. Enter the date
  8. Click send

Returning to Work

  • Inform Silke or Johannes that you are back
  • Revisit the web form and report yourself healthy
  • If you have questions or issues with the form, contact Silke or Johannes immediately
